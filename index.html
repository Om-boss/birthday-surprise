Our Journey

14 March 2026

The Day We Met

Our love story began with a simple eye contact as we crossed each other in the college corridor. Soon after, I sent you a follow request on Instagram, and you accepted it within just half an hour. I couldn't resist stalking your profile and ended up liking so many of your photos that you instantly noticed and sent me the unforgettable message, "Zala ka stalk karun account? 😂" That funny little conversation became the start of endless chats. Later, I asked you to join me for the fashion show, and from then on, we spent almost every day together, slowly growing closer and becoming deeply attached. We eagerly waited for Traditional Day just to see each other and create more beautiful memories. Looking back, it's incredible that our beautiful love story began with just one glance in a college corridor.

14 Mar 2026

Second Memory

One of our most special memories was our first long ride together on a two-wheeler to Mhasoba Mandir in Kharawade. Every moment of that journey felt magical, but what made it even more memorable was watching you confidently ride the Jupiter on unfamiliar roads with so much courage. Reaching the temple together and seeking the blessings of Lord Shiva made the day even more meaningful. It wasn't just a ride—it was the beginning of countless beautiful memories that we'll always cherish.

03 Mar 2026

Third Memory

Our first event together was Holi at Sunny's World, and it became one of the most unforgettable days of our journey. You looked absolutely beautiful, especially in your sunglasses, and every moment with you felt special. Dancing together in the rain dance, feeding each other, and celebrating our very first Holi made the day perfect. And of course, the funniest memory was when you changed into a different outfit in the car before coming out, only for your T-shirt to end up wet and covered in mud—and you were almost in tears while I couldn't stop laughing! 😂❤️

18 Mar 2026

Fourth Memory

One of my favorite memories from that day is our best photo together while we were dancing. We clicked nearly a hundred pictures just to get that one perfect shot, and in the end, the most beautiful one was taken naturally in the middle of the crowd. It was completely candid, filled with genuine happiness, and somehow became our favorite picture—a memory that perfectly captures the joy of being together. ❤️
